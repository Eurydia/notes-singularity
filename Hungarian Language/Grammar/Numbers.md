### Tens suffix

Apart from ten, twenty, and thirty, suffixes are used to represent the **tens-digits**.

The suffixes follow [[Consonants & Vowels#Vowels Harmony|vowels harmony]].

| -      | Mély & Végyes | Magas  |
| ------ | ------------- | ------ |
| Suffix | *-van*        | *-ven* |
